About me

I am a computational biologist having background of computer science. I am working as an associate professor at the department of computer science and engineering of the faculty of science and engineering in Waseda University. My research interests mainly center on algorithms for biological sequence analyses. My recent interest also includes privacy-preserving datamining for biological/biomedical data analyses.

Keywords

Computational biology, genomics, sequence analysis, next generation sequencing data, algorithms, data structure, machine learning, cryptographic protocol, privacy-preserving data mining
