The Computational Biology Laboratory (cBio Lab)

Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Faculty of Science and Engineering, Waseda Universiy

About

The cBio Lab has started in April 2016 at Waseda University. We are working on Computational Biology with the special focus on developing efficient methods for genomic data analysis.

Keywords

Computational biology, genomics, sequence analysis, next generation sequencing data, algorithms, data structure, machine learning, cryptographic protocol, privacy-preserving data mining
